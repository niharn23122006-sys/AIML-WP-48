Electric Vehicles Outsell Petrol Cars in Major Citie

Electric vehicles (EVs) have officially surpassed petrol car sales in major metropolitan cities worldwide. Government incentives and expanding charging infrastructure are driving this transformation.

Environmental analysts call this milestone a turning point in reducing urban pollution and carbon emissions.

Renewable Energy Surpasses Coal

For the first time in history, renewable energy sources have generated more electricity globally than coal-fired power plants. Solar and wind installations increased by 35% last year, driven by falling costs and government incentives. Environmental groups celebrate this as a crucial milestone in combating climate change.

Major telecommunications providers completed the nationwide rollout of 5G networks this month, bringing ultra-fast internet speeds to rural communities for the first time.
